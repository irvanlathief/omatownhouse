# Beaches Near Kaba Kaba

## Kedungu Beach (Closest Surf Beach)
- **Distance from Kaba Kaba**: ~10-15 minutes
- **Distance from Canggu**: ~30 minutes
- **Type**: Black volcanic sand beach
- **Rating**: 4.8/5 on TripAdvisor

### Surf Conditions
- Wave direction: Both left and right (A-frame)
- Best tide: Works at all tides, most comfortable mid to low
- Best swell: South, southwest, or west (3-8 ft ideal)
- Bottom: Mix of sand and reef
- Experience levels: Good for all, from beginners to advanced

### Three Main Breaks
1. **Main peak (A-frame)**: Right in front of warungs, both left and right waves - perfect for beginners
2. **Left-hand break**: Longer rides, works well at lower tides (watch for rocks, sea urchins, coral)
3. **Shore break ("Loloan")**: Short and steep, for advanced surfers only

### Facilities
- Wide parking lot
- Warungs (local restaurants)
- Showers and toilets
- Board rentals and instructors on beach
- Less crowded than Canggu

## Other Nearby Beaches

### Yeh Gangga Beach
- Rating: 4.0/5
- Black sand beach
- Good for sunset views

### Nyanyi Beach
- Tanah Lot's quieter cousin
- Great for sunset

### Balian Beach

### Pasut Beach
- Rating: 3.0/5

### Soka Beach
- Black sand beach

## Key Takeaways
- Tabanan beaches are hidden gems with less crowds
- Black sand beaches throughout
- Great surf spots for all levels
- Stunning sunset views
- More authentic Bali experience than Canggu/Seminyak


# Expat Lifestyle: Tabanan vs Canggu

## Why Tabanan is the "Next Canggu"

### Canggu Then (10 years ago)
- Quiet rice-field village
- Tranquil, rural, far from developed south Bali
- Affordable land
- Attracted expats, surfers, digital nomads

### Canggu Now
- Global hotspot
- Beach clubs, coworking spaces, cafes
- High-end villas
- Crowded, rising prices
- Losing original charm

### Tabanan Now
- Still largely natural: rice terraces, beaches, river valleys
- Land prices more moderate (IDR 6-12M/are inland, IDR 15-25M/are coastal)
- Developer interest growing
- Small resorts and boutique villas appearing

## Key Advantages of Tabanan

1. **Lower Property Prices** - Much cheaper than Canggu/Seminyak
2. **Large Plots Available** - More space for compounds/eco-resorts
3. **Untouched Natural Beauty** - Rice paddies, black sand beaches, waterfalls, jungle
4. **Growing Infrastructure** - Government pushing "Quality Tourism"
5. **Government Support** - Promoting regions beyond south Bali

## Lifestyle Appeal

### For Expats & Digital Nomads
- More privacy and space
- Lower cost of living vs Canggu
- Still within reach of Canggu amenities (20-30 min)
- Authentic Bali experience

### For Eco-Living & Wellness
- Wellness retreats viable
- Yoga spaces
- Eco-villas
- Sustainable tourism encouraged

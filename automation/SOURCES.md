# SOURCES: authority hierarchy for Bali off-plan real estate

Cite the highest tier available. Any number you publish (yield %, price, legal
duration, distance, development scale) needs a Tier 1 or Tier 2 source. Tier 3
is fine for context and color. Tier 4 may be linked as a venue target but never
used as a source for a fact.

## Tier 1 - authoritative (regulation, law, official data)

- Indonesian government and regulators: BKPM / OSS (investment, PT PMA),
  Ministry of ATR/BPN (land law, Hak Pakai, Hak Milik, leasehold), Tabanan
  Regency (Pemkab Tabanan) announcements and zoning.
- Bank Indonesia and BPS (Statistics Indonesia) for macro and currency data.
- Official development sources for named projects (for example nuanu.com for
  Nuanu Creative City scale and facilities).

## Tier 2 - strong industry

- Established Bali agencies and their market reports (for example Bali Property
  Group, Exotiq Property, Kibarer, Harcourts Purba Bali).
- International advisers with Indonesia coverage: Knight Frank, Savills,
  Colliers, JLL.
- Reputable expat legal and company-setup firms: Emerhub, Cekindo, ILA Global
  Consulting (good for PT PMA, leasehold vs freehold, foreign ownership).

## Tier 3 - context and color

- Named developments and operators (Alila / Hyatt, Ulaman, hotel brand press).
- Major travel and lifestyle press (for venues, neighbourhoods, beaches).
- Established expat publications (for example The Bali Sun, Coconuts Bali) for
  recent local news, corroborated where it states a hard fact.

## Tier 4 - link only, do not cite as fact

- Forums, Reddit, individual blogs, and social media. Instagram and Google Maps
  links are acceptable as venue link targets only.

## Verification rule

If you cannot find a Tier 1 or Tier 2 source for a numeric or legal claim, do
not publish that claim. Choose a different angle or mark the topic `blocked` in
topics.json with a short reason.
